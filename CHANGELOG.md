## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-31

### Added
- Conversational AI portfolio — talk to an avatar that answers questions and navigates the
  site by voice instead of clicking through static pages.
- Manga/anime visual design across the hero, About, Skills, ElevenHacks, Education, and Contact.
- Standalone pages for each of the eleven ElevenHacks builds, deep-linkable by URL.
- Ambient background audio and click feedback with a mute toggle.
- Custom cursor with a fading code trail.
